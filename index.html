Book of They

About BoT

The Book of They is a book detailing the works of They.

The idea is that words connect on one level, sentences on another, paragraphs upon another, chapters on another, until finally books on another.

Connections

  1. Words are the chemical makeup.
  2. Sentences is the product of that makeup.
  3. Paragraphs are ideas.
  4. Chapters direct those ideas.
  5. Books apply those ideas.
